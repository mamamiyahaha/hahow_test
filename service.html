品牌設計 BRANDING DESIGN

#標誌 #識別規範 #形象廣告 #包裝設計
品牌設計包含的範圍非常廣，很多人或許認為只是一個標誌，其實不然，品牌是從無形的概念、精神與價值開始往外延伸，化為有形的視覺標誌，再延伸至各種與企業或品牌的事務用品、宣傳品等...做一致性的溝通，對內可以凝聚組織共識，對外創造信任感。所以，需要有嚴謹與專業的流程來擬定與執行。我們擅長從各種面向彙整分類出好的設計關鍵點，並運用創意溝通，協助品牌深入市場。

#logo #identification standards #image advertising #package design
The scope of branding design is rather extensive. It is not simply a logo as believed by many people. The idea of branding is to extend intangible concepts, spirits and values outward to transform them into a tangible visual logo; and then have the scope of applications thereof extended to any business or promotional supplies related to the corporation or brand for consistent communications. As branding helps the company to reach consensus internally and create a sense of trust externally, it requires cautious and professional drafting and implementation processes. We are good at summarizing and classifying good design points; and using our creativity and communication skills to assist brands penetrating the market.

空間設計 INTERIOR DESIGN

#室內居家 #商業空間 #展示會場 #陳列空間
空間設計是所有設計類別裡與「人」的緊密度最高也最直接的，當人們進入一個空間或會場，它的份圍與設計，將決定該空間與人的連結程度，所以我們在空間設計非常注重人的感受，以及媒材的使用；人可以透過感受空間的動線、光線、聲音及媒材等，影響決策或思考，進而策動人在這個空間裡的作為，就像古代孟母三遷的道理一樣，環境很重要。

#indoor home #commercial space #exhibition venue #shelf space
Among all design categories, interior design is the closest and most directly connected to “people”. When people entering a space or venue, the atmosphere and design thereof will decide how it is connected to people. We therefore pay great attention to people’s feelings and media that we use when designing a space. People can have their decisions or thinking model changed when experiencing the traffic flow, lights, sounds and media of a space. Such change will further motivate people’s behavior in the space. Like the story of how Mencius' mother moved her home three times to better her son's education, environment is very important.

網頁設計 WEB DESIGN

#企業網站 #活動網站 #影片 #廣告BANNER #eDM
網路已經變成這個世紀取得資訊很重要的工具，雖不是唯一，卻像反射動作一樣，常常成為首選，因此，網路變成為人們認識企業或品牌的第一個管道，如何透過網站、eDM或是廣告BANNER，讓閱覽者在最短的時間「信任」企業或品牌，便是開啟合作甚至消費行為的關鍵。我們洞察網路閱讀行為及心理，透過方法分析，找出最適合業主的溝通方式，協助打造最佳的線上形象與溝通策略。

#corporate website #event website #video #advertising banner #eDM
Internet has already become an important tool for acquiring information in the century. Despite not being the only tool, it has often become the top choice like a reflex action. Therefore, internet has become the first channel through which people get to know the company or brand. How to make readers trust a company or brand without the shortest time using website, eDM or advertising BANNER is the key to initiate cooperation or consuming behavior. With in-depth insights into internet reading behavior and psychology, we are able to, through method analysis, find a communication method that is most suitable to the customer to assist it in building the best on-line image and adopting the optimum communication strategy.

活動場佈 EVENT DECORATION

#活動視覺 #記者會 #研討會 #展覽
活動視覺設計及執行需要極具同理心，一般活動常常會需要對應不同窗口，並掌控時效，若沒有運用同理心的去評斷客戶及廠商狀況，很容造成誤會並出現溝通斷層，影響活動前置作業的進行。因此，伯納除了以此教育設計外，在製作物溝通上，活動後期我們同時會讓設計直接對應客戶，以利更加瞭解客戶的需求，並有效率的執行，這是我們一直以來的文化：技術為本、服務為人、效率為事。

#event visual design #press conference #seminar #exhibition
An event visual design and the implementation thereof requires a high degree of empathy because it often requires contacts with various windows and control the project effectiveness within a specific time frame. When judging customer or supplier’s conditions without empathy, misunderstanding and even communication gaps can easily occur, affecting pre-event preparations. Therefore, BORNA not only educates our designers, but also, with respect to the production of point of sale materials, lets our designers to directly contact with the customers late of this event to better understand customers’ needs and efficient implement the project. This is our long-established culture: oriented to technology, serving for people and accomplishment with efficiency.

印刷設計 PRINT DESIGN

#海報 #DM #手冊 #型錄 #卡片
印刷是將設計師無形的概念轉化成有型的物品，所有的印刷條件：特殊材質、銅版紙、模造紙、特別色及各種印刷特效，都必須經由設計安排，再透過與印刷工廠的合作默契，最後才呈現到業主手中。對伯納而言，設計從無形的概念到有型的印刷條件，都必須恰如其分的存在，才能透過印刷設計為成果加分！

#poster #DM #brochure #catalog #card
The idea of printing is to transform designers’ intangible concepts into tangible objects. All the printing conditions, including the use of special material, art paper, simile paper, special colors and all kinds of special printing effects, must be arranged by the designer. Then, relying on the tacit understanding of our cooperative printing factory, the design is put into production and given to the customer. For BORNA, intangible design conditions and tangible printings conditions must be taken care of in order to produce wonderful results!
